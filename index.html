Into the lap of Nature

The green trees, the sky, the mountains, the rain, and many more natural and magical things are gifts to humanity.

  • Whenever I am surrounded by nature, I feel so relaxed and peaceful. My favourite season is Winter Season. The cool breeze, the mountains covered with snow, the warm sunset, etc. feels like heaven.
  • For me, staring at the sky, walking in the garden is like an escape from reality.
  • I like taking pictures of trees, sunset and skies, the birds and the squirrels.
  • Besides the beauty of nature, it has some great importance too.

    Importance of Nature:

  • Provides essential resources
  • Supports biodiversity
  • Regulates climate
  • Improves mental and physical health


Books: The True Friends

The Books are the truest friends a person can have.

  • Books teach you. They have the power to change you, in some way that you don't realize immediately, but after some time.
  • A habit of reading benefits you for life. You can join a reading club, or read alone, as you prefer. I prefer reading alone.. on my rooftop at evening.
  • I like reading books about life, science and my religion.

    My Favourite Books:

  • 'Bhagvadgita'
  • 'Karma' by Sadhguru
  • 'The Power of Subconscious Mind' by Joseph Murphy

'The Power of Subconscious Mind' is a very popular and great book on the subconscious power of our mind, that can do and has done wonders in people's life. If you haven't read it then I suggest you to read it. I bet you will love it!



Let's dive into Music!

Music has the power to make us feel different kinds of emotions, and everyone likes music, don't they?

  • Music is diverse. Every culture and region has its own kind of music, and instruments. I like to listen to songs in Hindi, English, Punjabi, Urdu, Korean, etc. languages and instrumental music.
  • Music is like a therapy. The calm music, vocals makes me feel relaxed and free. I like to listen to sad songs, with a mix of calm and high pitch vocals. It doesn't make me feel sad but happy and free, for some unknown reason.
